About us


Established in 1997, SS LEATHERS, is a leading tanner and exporter of finished leather based in Tamil Nadu, India.
The company manufactures a wide range of leathers, in an assortment of finishes, optics, thicknesses, physical and performance properties from sheepskin of Indian origin, for gloving, apparel, leather goods and shoe industries.
At it's foundation is a technically qualified and professionally competent team, which oversees and implements strict quality control procedures from beam-house to finishing.
Keen attention to application technology, process and product development for more than 27 years have synchronised the companyâ€™s product line to be compliant with international market standards, requirements and norms.
SS Leathers has an annual capacity for manufacturing and exporting 3.5 millions sq ft of sheep nappa. SS Leathers exports its finished leather to reputed factories in China, Hong Kong, Korea, South Africa and Japan.
SS Leathers is a quality conscious company. It strives to offer its clients a balance of product, delivery and price, complemented with ethical business policy, transparency and accountability.

Infrastructure


SS LEATHERS possesses a state-of-the-art facility, equipped with modern plant for leather processing.
The vertically integrated operation encompasses the entire leather production process, from raw skin sourcing to finishing, enabling stringent quality control at every stage.

Leathers

Finished Leather
SS LEATHERS specialises in the production of meticulously finished leather, marked by superior craftsmanship and adherence to international quality standards. Our finished leather stands out for their exceptional softness, durability, and versatility, making these their preferred choice for manufacturing products of highest integrity durability and quality.
Shoe Leather
Our shoe leather collection is presented with precision to meet the demanding requirements of the footwear industry. Known for its physicality, flexibility, density, and aesthetic appeal, our shoe leather enhances the quality of footwear, ensuring both style and wear comfort.
Glove Leather
Designed to meet the unique demands of glove manufacturing, our gloveleather is recognized for its supple texture and excellent tactility andstretch. Careful selection of materials and precision in processing makeour glove leather the ideal choice for premium hand-wear.
Garment Leather
Our garment leather combines style and substance, offering a perfect blend of comfort and fashion. Renowned for its softness, drape and silky natural hand-feel qualities, and durability, these garment leather add a touch of luxury to a wide range of apparel, setting new standards in the fashion industry.
Leather for Goods
SS LEATHERS provides high-quality leather for various goods, ensuring longevity and aesthetic appeal. From bags to accessories, our leather for goods is crafted to attain the diverse needs of manufacturers, ensuring a perfect balance of durability and style.
